About Us

Our Story

"Greyjoy Bakery" sprang to life from Hitesh's lifelong passion for baking, nurtured by his grandmother's expertise. He started by selling his delectable creations at local farmers' markets, eventually securing a small business loan to open the bakery in Ottawa's neighborhood. With his dedication, family support, and a love for sharing his treats, the bakery quickly became a cherished community gem.
